FAQs

How can I book a self drive car?

You can book a car by giving us a call or sending a message on whatsapp. We also have online booking process which you can use to book Goa car rent, give us a call and we will walk you through it.

How much do you charge for pickup from Dabolim Airport?

Absolutely Zero additional cost for that. We don’t charge for pickup from Dabolim Airport. It is advisable to make your car rental at Goa airport booking in advance to avoid last time hassle.

How much Self drive car companies charge for car Pickup from Goa Dabolim Airport?

Generally a lot of car providers charge in a range of 500 INR to 1500 INR, but if you are our customer you have the privilege to get it totally free.

What will happen if I return my car later than the decided drop-off time.

Generally we are very lineant with our customers so we do allow some extra margin time depending on the availablity of that car. However sometimes It’s not possible specially during high season time due to back to back bookings. Please give us a call to know how availability of that car looks like.

Does your company also provide pickup and drop at new Mopa airport?

Mopa airport is located in Pernem taluka, North Goa and the newest addition to boost the tourism in Goa. Our company provide pickup and drop services on demand basis for our customers when it comes to serve at car rental at Mopa Airport area. Give us a call today.
